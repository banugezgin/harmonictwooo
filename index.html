The Second Harmonic & Opposition

Astrology, with its different type of harmonics, unveils unique energies within each individual's birth chart. Among these, the second harmonic takes center stage, portraying an opposition. In this article, we delve into the realm of the second harmonic, exploring its principles, sub-harmonics, and the subtle nuances that shape our astrological interpretitions.

Picture it as a cosmic cconflict between 'Me - You,' 'Good - Bad,' and 'Love - Hate.' Alternatively, these opposing forces can harmonise, but presently, they exist in separate realms—a principle of separation.

This opposition demands effort—an active pursuit of goals and aspirations. While some may perceive the second harmonic as a source of stress and challenge, its robust energy serves as a potential force to defeat life's difficulties.

Sub Harmonics

Second harmonic principle extends its reach into sub-harmonics, each revealing a layer of complexity within an individual's astrological makeup.

  1. 4th Harmonic (2x2): This harmonic exposes conflicting aims within an individual's nature. The energy between two planets can be consciously channeled for constructive purposes.
  2. 8th Harmonic (2x2x2): Operating at a deeper, subconscious level, this harmonic, along with others below, encompasses square qualities. Unraveling these layers requires an exploration of the individual's hidden depths.
  3. 16th Harmonic (2x2x2x2): Representing the sesquiquadrate aspect, the 16th and 32nd harmonics may be overlooked unless part of a specific pattern in the second harmonic. When identified, they unveil critical aspects of the chart owner's nature.
  4. 32nd Harmonic (2x2x2x2x2): A unique quality in harmonic astrology, especially impactful with close orbs, this harmonic sheds light on crucial 2nd harmonic patterns within a birth chart.

Understanding internal disputes within an individual's character requires a deep examination of respective planet positions in the chart, considering both houses and signs.
